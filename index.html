De dónde venimos

Our posturings, our imagined self-importance,
the delusion that we have some privileged
position in the Universe, are challenged
by this point of pale light.

Our planet is a lonely speck in the great
enveloping cosmic dark. In our obscurity, in all
this vastness, there is no hint that help will
come from elsewhere to save us from ourselves.
